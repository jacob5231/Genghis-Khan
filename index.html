Genghis Khan - The Great Conqueror

Who Was Genghis Khan?

Genghis Khan, born as Temujin, was the founder of the Mongol Empire, the largest contiguous empire in history. He united the Mongol tribes and led them to conquer vast lands, spreading influence from China to Europe. His leadership and strategies are still studied today.

Empire Building

Genghis Khan's empire-building strategies involved both military conquest and diplomacy. He formed alliances with neighboring tribes and employed skilled generals and tactics. His legacy as a warrior and ruler remains unparalleled.
